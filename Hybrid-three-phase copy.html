Flexible and Scalable Solutions

The LIVOLTEK HP3-5K/6K/8K/10K/12K/15K/17K/20K/25K/30K D1 hybrid inverter offers flexible and scalable solutions for both residential and commercial applications. It provides scalable battery storage, facilitating powerful energy backup, and high self-consumption with optimized built-in EMS to reduce energy costs.

Advanced Features

Features include 150% PV oversize, 100% unbalanced output, and max. 20A DC input current per string. The inverter ensures free online monitoring and maintenance, UPS level switching time for critical loads, and multiple working modes for optimal performance.

Wide Voltage Range

The inverter operates within a voltage range of 150V-800V, providing flexibility for different installation scenarios. It ensures efficient energy backup and self-consumption for residential and commercial applications.

Multi-Working Modes

The inverter supports multi-working modes for optimal performance, adapting to varying energy demands. Its modular scalable design allows you to start small and expand as your residential or commercial energy storage needs grow.
