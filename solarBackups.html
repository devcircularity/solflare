How Solaris Kit Works

Discover the Intelligent Features of the SolarisKit Solar Collector

1

Sun Enters Solar Prism

SolarisKit solar prisms are strategically positioned in sunny areas (e.g., roofs). The unique prismatic shape enables passive tracking of the sun.

2

Absorption of Sunlight

Solar radiation is absorbed by the SolarisKit solar collector absorber, increasing its temperature. Internal aluminum reflectors direct sunlight onto the absorber.

3

Cold Water In

Cold water is circulated through the solar collector using a small pump. As water travels through the absorber, it is heated via forced convection.

4

Hot Water Out

Carbon-free hot water then exits from the collector via the outlet port fitting. Metric and imperial size fittings are available to suit your needs.
